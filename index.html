Rectangular Zip Pouch Generator

Zip pouches are perfect for organizing those errant items that always end up dropping to the bottom of your pack, things like headlamp, spare batteries, and chapstick. Zip sacks are great projects for beginners as well as making something functional from large scraps.

Input your desired zip pouch dimensions to find the exact fabric size with seam allowances and instructions.
