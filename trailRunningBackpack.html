Small hybrid running backpack with roll top and compression straps.

Vestlike stretch harness with bottle pockets.

Seamless front panel for weather resistance and durability.

Roll top closure with various closure options.

Whether trail running, fastpacking, or summit bagging, a versatile backpack is essential for full day outings. Designed to blend the best features of modern running vests with the capacity and durability of alpine packs, moving fast on exposed terrain has never been more comfortable.
