Do It All Shorts

All summer. All wear. DIAS

Features

First released in 1982, Patagonia® Baggies™ have been a summer staple for 40 years. Inspired by the classics, but with a modern shake, let DIAS be your next make.

Shorts fit for all summer sports
  • Classic style outdoor shorts with regular rise and casual straight fit
  • Deep hand pockets that won't flip out and with an inner vertical iPhone sleeve
  • One or two back pockets with or without a secure snap flap
  • Full length leg gusset for range of motion and squat comfort
  • Drawcord and elastic waistband constructed to allow for easy replacement
Sewing Skill Level - Intermediate Beginner
While a beginner can make this project, familiarity with sewing terms and construction techniques is expected. Whether overlocking raw edges or flat felling all seams, construction is straightforward. Serging is optional but recommended for fastest construction. This is a great beginner garment pattern with low-cost fabric options. Make a prototype and have fun!

Fabrics

For the classic look and feel, Supplex Nylon aka Taslan with DWR gives fast drying and long lasting shorts also with UPF sun protection! 2-ply Supplex make great running shorts but lighter colors can be too sheer. 4-ply is most durable and closer weight to the original Patagonia shorts. If wanting a little stretch, look for 5-10% spandex content. Also don't be afraid to try poly blends or natural fabric options too! A few trusted online shops:

  • The Rainshed is a favorite shop for Supplex in tons of colors
  • Discovery Fabrics offers many stretch wovens in various colors and weights
