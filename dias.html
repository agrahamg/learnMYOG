Sewing Skill Level - Intermediate Beginner

While a beginner can make this project, familiarity with sewing terms and construction techniques is expected. Whether overlocking raw edges or flat felling all seams, construction is straightforward. Serging is optional but recommended for fastest construction. DIAS are a great beginner wearable pattern thats low complexity and uses modest materials.
