Fabrics

For the classic look and feel, Supplex Nylon aka Taslan with DWR will be fast drying and long lasting shorts. Supplex is often high UPF sun protection rated! 2-ply Supplex make great running or paddleboard shorts, but lighter colors can be a bit sheer. 4-ply is most durable and feels to be a closer weight to the original PatagoniaÂ® Baggiesâ„¢. If wanting a little stretch, look for 5-10% lycra or spandex content. Also don't be afraid to try poly-blends or natural fabric options too! A few trusted online shops are listed below.

  • The Rainshed is a favorite shop for Supplex in many colors, finishes, and three common weights
  • Quest Outfitters has a nice color selection of lightweight Supplex/Taslan
  • Discovery Fabrics offers many stretch wovens in various colors and weights. Check out 'Surfer' for lightweight stretch woven with DWR.
