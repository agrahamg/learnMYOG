Body Measurements and Fit Guide

The Pullover Hoodie is a casual fit with 3-4 inches (7.5-10 cm) of design ease. Body and final garment measurements are listed. Fit is between a relaxed and slim fit.

Beta Series patterns are tested and complete sewing patterns, available early to the community to gether feedback. While a beginner can make this project, familiarity with sewing terms and construction techniques is required. For best results when sewing knits or high pile fleece, a serger or domestic machine with satin (zig zag) stitch is necessary. Grading between sizes and/or pattern adjustments may be required for perfect fit.

Fabrics

Technical apparel fabrics used by big brands are tough to source by the yard. I recommend making your first Pullover Hoodie with "Anti-Pill Plush Fleece" from JOANN or other craft store.
When you've nailed the pattern and construction, Polartec fleece is much higher quality, more comfortable, and more durable. Suggested sources of Polartec fleeces include The Rain Shed and Ebay stores Discount Fabric 1 and Jens_Buys

Fabric Panel Options Length (Width)
Main Body Anti-pill Fleece, Polartec Thermal Pro, Polartec 200 Fleece 2 yd (58")
Pocket Lining Wicking knit (DryFit), taslan or supplex, poly blend 1/2 yd (45")
